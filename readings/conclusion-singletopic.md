# Questions

End your reflection with two lists of questions:

**Write one or two questions you are comfortable answering after reading**

**Write one or two questions you think are important to be able to answer after reading this paper, but which you cannot yet.**

After these questions, include a brief statement that you did not use an LLM (like ChatGPT) _or_ a brief description of how you used it – e.g., to revise your own writing, or to prepare your initial draft. **If you use ChatGPT, share a link to your chat discussions within the reflection.** My goal is for you to engage with the readings. Using ChatGPT to generate your entire reading reflection is allowed but **not** encouraged – and unlikely to work well!

Please feel free to link to other papers or blog articles that you referenced while considering the reading.
